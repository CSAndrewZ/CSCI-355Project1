Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order?

There are multiple ways to order from CMZ Pizzeria.

  • You can order through our Website by selecting the menu items.
  • You can place an order through a phone call
  • You can walkin to our store and place an order.

What kind of payments do you accept?

We accept all types of payments. This includes through credit/debit card, Apple Pay, or cash for store walkin

Can I order online without an account?

Yes, we allow guest checkout on all orders. We do recommend creating an account to save all of your order info, exclusive deals, for quicker checkout, and reorder from a few clicks for our enjoyable fresh pizza!

Do you offer discounts or special promotions?

Yes we do, every week and during special holidays we will have deals/promotions shown in our front page!

How do I change or cancel an order?

Once you placed an order, the details is sent immediately to the restaurant. You should call the restaurant right away if you need to modify or cancel the order. We can be reached at the number:718-453-1234

What is your most popular pizza?

Our most popular slices of pizza is about Pepperoni Pizza, it is our and its mutual feeling among our past customers.

Do you plan on opening more locations?

Yes we plan on opening more locations in the near future for everyone to experience our fresh pizza!
